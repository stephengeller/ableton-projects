CLIP-TO-ZERO

Clip to zero. Properly.

A macOS audio plugin for the clip-to-zero workflow. Auto-Gain to target, four clip curves, ITU-R BS.1770-4 LUFS and 4× oversampled true-peak metering, optional oversampling around the clipper, scrolling oscilloscope with pre/post diff.
VST3 + AU + Standalone, universal Apple Silicon + Intel.

THE WORKFLOW

1

Stage to 0

Auto-Gain captures the peak over a 2 second window and stages input to your target dBFS. Drag the knob manually if you'd rather.

2

Drive into clipper

Push Drive into the ceiling. Hard, Soft, Poly, or Tube curves. Oversample at 2× / 4× / 8× to keep aliasing out. Pre-clip HPF to protect headroom.

3

Judge by LUFS

Momentary, Short-term, and Integrated loudness per BS.1770-4. Crest factor, true-peak readout, and gain-matched A/B bypass so you compare fairly.

WHAT'S INSIDE
